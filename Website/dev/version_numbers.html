Version Numbers in JPhotoTagger

The version number of JPhotoTagger has 3 parts (numbers), where the first two are related to the features and the third to bug fixes:

  1. The first number is the major number and remains zero until stability and usability are good enough for 1 and higher. Then it is incremented only on "major" changes by 1.
  2. The second number is the minor number and counted up by 1 if new features were added.
  3. The third number will be used for bug fixes of a special version (increments: 1).
