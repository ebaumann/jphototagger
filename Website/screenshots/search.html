Screenshots: Advanced Search

With the advanced search You can define custom queries and save them. Examples are: Finding images with my daughter until the age of 10 or finding landscape images showing all of that: a tree, a river and a mountain. The advanced search contains three registers, adequate for beginners to to experts.

A mouse click on an image displays it's original size.
